Straight answers

The questions you're already asking.

Isn't this just spammy mass email?
No. Doot researches each contact and writes for that specific person, then sends at human volume from a properly set-up domain with a clear opt-out. The whole point is to protect your sender reputation, not burn it.
Do I have to switch tools?
No. Doot works with the inbox and CRM you already use. And you approve every message before it goes out — until you trust it enough to let it run on its own.
How fast can it start?
A first batch can be live in days, not months. No job posting, no three-month ramp, no onboarding a new hire.
What does it cost?
We scope it to your pipeline on the call — most teams start with a small setup and a monthly run, far below the cost of a single SDR hire. You'll get a clear number, not a runaround.
Who's behind Doot?
An engineer from IIT Bombay who builds agentic AI systems for a living. You'll talk to the person who builds your agent — not an account manager.
